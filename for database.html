About HU Swimming pool

Welcome to the Haramaya University Swimming Pool, your premier destination for recreation, fitness, and community engagement on campus. Nestled within the picturesque landscapes of Haramaya University, our swimming pool offers a refreshing oasis for students, faculty, and staff to unwind, exercise, and connect with others. At Haramaya University Swimming Pool, we believe in promoting a culture of health and wellness among our university community. Our state-of-the-art facilities are designed to cater to a wide range of aquatic activities, from leisurely swims to competitive training sessions. Whether you're a seasoned athlete or a beginner looking to improve your swimming skills, our pool provides the perfect setting for you to achieve your fitness goals.
